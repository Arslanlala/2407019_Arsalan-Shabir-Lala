About

The Solar System formed 4.6 billion years ago from the gravitational collapse of a giant interstellar molecular cloud.

Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System. It is a nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma.

Moons

There are over 200 known moons orbiting the planets and dwarf planets in the Solar System.
